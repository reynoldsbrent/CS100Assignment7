Steve Wozniak

Dates: 08/11/1950 - Present

Significant Contribution

Steve Wozniak is an American electronics engineer and cofounder of Apple Computer alongside Steve Jobs. He was the designer of the first commercially successful PC.

Biography

Steve Wozniak was born on August 11, 1950 in San Jose, California. He atended the University of Colorado at Boulder for one year before he dropped out. During the early 1970s, hw worked at a number of small electronics firms in San Francisco and eventually obtained a position at Hewlett-Packard Company in 1975. Wozniak also attended the Homebrew Computer CLub where he and Steve Jobs worked together to found Apple.
